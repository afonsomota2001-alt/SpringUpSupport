SpringUp Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

SpringUp respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

1. Who we are

SpringUp is a training management app for trampoline and DMT, built for coaches and athletes. The data controller is the app developer, reachable through the email address listed at the end of this document.

2. Data we collect

We only collect the data required for the app to function:

  • Account: name, email address, and password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Profile: role (coach or athlete), date of birth, height, weight, and resting heart rate (optional)
  • Training data: sessions, turns, routines, scores, time of flight (TOF), and performance notes
  • Competitions: results and rankings entered manually
  • Internal communication: notes sent by coaches to athletes inside the app
  • Notifications: push notification token to send app alerts (optional)

We do not collect GPS location data, we do not access your camera or microphone, and we do not collect browsing data or any data not listed above.

3. How we use data

  • Enable account and app access
  • Display training history, dashboards, and statistics
  • Allow coaches to monitor athlete progress
  • Send training-related push notifications (if allowed)

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Storage and security

Data is stored on Supabase servers (infrastructure in the European Union). Communication is handled exclusively over HTTPS. Passwords are stored in encrypted form and never in plain text.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access.

5. Data sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties, is not sold, and is not used for advertising or any purpose other than operating the app.

6. Data retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time using the contact email below.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the email below.

8. Minors

The app may be used by minor athletes under the supervision of their coach or legal guardian. Minors' data is handled with the same level of protection as all other users. Minor registration should be done with the knowledge of a parent or guardian.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be changed. Significant changes will be communicated inside the app.
